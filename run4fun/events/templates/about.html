-About us-

Our Mission

We collect sport event information, both upcoming and past, and strive to combine these existing dispersed databases under the same service portal. Our mission is to present this data in a meaningful and visually modern way and in such manner that browsing sport event results is moderately fun and professional at the same time. Finding desired results relevant to user must be easy, fast and to be done feeling comfortable from the beginning of the use of the result service until the goal is reached, and visit on the site will end. Also, we want to provide such a beautiful and elegant site, that user do not want to close our page even after the desired information has gained ;).

Vision

Since we know that there are a lot of enthusiastic athletes and data lovers we do not want to only show your interval and end time of the competition, but also how is your progress over longer period. In our future vision users of the service can have their own personal user profile and check progress themself with comprehensive graphical and statistical feedback. The comparison is an integral part of the competition, not necessarily to others, but to athlete himself and his own previous performance. But don't worry, we are creative for you and our promise to you is to find nice, fun and very pleasant information from all your past competitions and combine them in such way that you are going to be impressed, even if you have not the greatest passion for data. Let's do it!!
